About TIH

TIH- The Innovation Hub 2K19 is an organization which has core value to Develop Curios generation from any aspects.
OR
TIH is the group or organization of the students of all multidisciplinary branches of technical and Non technical fields who want to increase, improve and develop new skills in them and want to do something new innovative and extraordinary work in their life. We at the TIH work together to improve our entire team members and help all the members in the way they want and update the members with new trends in technology. The main aim of the TIH is to create a Hub or platform of students where students get all the necessary Research details, help, and guidance they want to develop and innovate something new and extraordinary in their interesting domain.
